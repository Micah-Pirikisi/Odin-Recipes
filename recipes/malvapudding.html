Malva Pudding

Malva pudding is a traditional South African dessert known for its sweet, spongy texture and rich caramel flavor. It's typically served warm with a generous drizzle of hot cream or custard.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon apricot jam
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons butter (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the sugar, flour, and salt.
  3. Add the milk, vinegar, apricot jam, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. In a small bowl, dissolve the baking soda in a tablespoon of warm water and add it to the batter. Mix well.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased baking dish.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. While the pudding is baking, prepare the sauce by heating 1 cup of cream with 1/2 cup of sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  8. Once the pudding is done, remove it from the oven and immediately pour the hot sauce over it.
  9. Let it soak for a few minutes before serving warm with extra cream or custard if desired.
